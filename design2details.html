Delicious Burger Brunch Ad


Design Concept & Inspiration:

This advertisement design was crafted for a college project in 2023, focusing on promoting a burger brunch menu. The goal of the design was to create a visually appealing, mouth-watering advertisement that draws attention and encourages immediate action. The design captures the essence of a fast-food promotion with a playful, bold aesthetic.

Visual Elements:

  • 1.Background & Color Scheme: The dominant use of bright oranges and yellows creates a warm and energetic atmosphere, immediately grabbing attention. These colors are often associated with appetite stimulation, making them perfect for a food advertisement. The vibrant gradient from yellow to orange provides a sense of depth while maintaining a clean and lively feel.
  • 2.Typography: The bold, playful typography in "DELICIOUS BURGER BRUNCH" is designed to be eye-catching and easy to read. The word "BURGER" is emphasized with a larger size and slightly curvier font to match the fun, energetic feel of the design. The combination of yellow and white font colors contrasts beautifully with the orange background, ensuring the text stands out.
  • 3.Burger Images: The central focus of the design is the three stacked burgers, positioned front and center. These burgers are rendered in high detail, with juicy patties, melted cheese, pickles, and condiments that look realistic and appetizing. The slightly overlapping placement of the burgers adds dimension, making them look even more tempting. The soft, golden buns suggest freshness and quality, enhancing the overall appeal.
  • 4.Call to Action: The "ORDER NOW" button, displayed in bright orange with a clear arrow icon, invites immediate action. The contrast between the button’s orange background and the white text makes it pop on the page, making it easy for viewers to know how to engage with the brand.
  • 5.Contact Information & Website:Beneath the call-to-action button, the design features a clear phone number and website address for ordering. The text is clean and accessible, ensuring that viewers know exactly how to get in touch or place their orders.
  • 6.Free Delivery Icon:The "FREE DELIVERY" badge is cleverly incorporated with a truck icon, adding another persuasive element to the design. It creates an additional incentive for customers, subtly enhancing the value proposition.
  • 7.Logo:The "BURGERS DELICIOUS" logo is positioned in the upper right-hand corner, neatly framing the design. Its use of a burger icon reinforces brand identity while maintaining the casual, fun tone of the overall layout.

Color Palette: The combination of warm tones—such as yellows and oranges—creates a sense of urgency and hunger. These colors are proven to stimulate appetite and add vibrancy to the composition. The subtle shadows beneath the burgers enhance realism and make the product the star of the design.

Tools & Techniques: The design was created using Adobe Photoshop, utilizing techniques such as layering, blending modes, shadowing, and masking to create a professional, polished look. The high-quality images and bold typography work in harmony to produce a cohesive advertisement.

Purpose & Impact: This ad is tailored for a fast-food restaurant aiming to increase sales during brunch hours. The vibrant design, delicious imagery, and clear call to action make it ideal for digital platforms like social media, as well as print. It speaks directly to hungry consumers and effectively drives engagement with its clear messaging and attractive visuals.
