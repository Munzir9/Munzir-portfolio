Travel Promotional Design

This promotional design was created as part of a college project in 2023 using Adobe Photoshop. The objective was to develop a visually engaging travel advertisement for a fictional travel agency that would attract potential customers through a clean, modern layout and bold design elements.

Key Design Features:

  • 1.Vibrant Colors and Contrast:The design uses bright blue and yellow as the primary colors, symbolizing energy, adventure, and optimism, which resonate well with the theme of travel. The contrast between blue and yellow helps key promotional messages stand out, creating an eye-catching and inviting design.
  • 2.Bold Typography: The typography plays a crucial role in guiding the viewer's attention. Large, bold fonts are used for the most important elements like “Promo $999” and “Book Now,” making it clear and easy to follow the call to action.
  • 3.Imagery:Two images have been integrated into the design. The background image shows a traveler waiting at a train station, suggesting the start of a journey. The circular image of a smiling tourist adds a personal touch, making the ad feel more relatable and human-centric.
  • 4.Icons for Key Offerings: The design includes three simple icons representing planes, locations, and photoshoots. These icons quickly communicate the key services provided, reinforcing the message without overwhelming the viewer with too much text.
  • 5.Call to Action::The phrase "Book Now" is prominently featured in a bright yellow box on the right side of the design, encouraging immediate action. The easy-to-read URL and social media icons provide additional channels for engagement.
  • 6.Balanced Layout:The overall layout is balanced and follows a clear structure, making the information easy to digest. The use of circular elements in the top section provides a soft contrast to the sharp edges in the rest of the design, adding a visually pleasing dynamic to the composition.
  • 7.Target Audience:The design targets young travelers looking for affordable travel experiences. The $999 promotional price is placed prominently in the layout to immediately capture the interest of budget-conscious consumers.

Tools & Techniques:

Software: Adobe Photoshop
Techniques: Layering, color correction, typography adjustments, iconography design, and image blending.

This design showcases my ability to create professional, commercial advertisements using modern design principles, making it an essential addition to my portfolio as a demonstration of my skills in both layout design and marketing communication.
